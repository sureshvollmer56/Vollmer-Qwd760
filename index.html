🧯 URGENT — Fire Extinguisher Cylinder Ships EMPTY — CO₂ Must Be Filled Before Commissioning

The CO₂ fire extinguisher cylinder is shipped empty and disconnected for safe transport. Before fixing the cylinder back into the machine, the following steps are mandatory:

  1. Take the empty cylinder to an authorised CO₂ refilling station and fill it to the specified charge weight
  2. Weigh the filled cylinder and record the gross weight — this is your reference for future inspections
  3. Mark the cylinder with the date of refill and filled weight (gross weight including gas)

⚠ Machine CANNOT be commissioned or operated without a fully charged and properly installed fire extinguisher.
